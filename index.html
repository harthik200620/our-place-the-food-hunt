Our Story · Since 2018

A riverside village,
set for dinner

"Some restaurants serve dinner. We wanted to give Vijayawada an evening — water, birdsong, woodsmoke and a proper Andhra table."

Across the Varadhi, behind a rock waterfall, Our Place spreads over a lantern-lit garden of thatched huts, pergolas and koi ponds. The kitchen keeps one foot in the village — raagi sangati, natu kodi, the clay tandoor — and one in the city's favourites, from dum biryanis to Indo-Chinese.

Come for a birthday, a family Sunday, or just a long unhurried dinner under the lights — there's a corner here shaped for it.

— The Our Place family

Spread across a walled garden between farm fields, Our Place isn't a restaurant you drop into — it's an evening out. Pick your corner: a thatched hut, a plant-hung pergola, the koi zone, or the industrial-rustic indoor hall with the full bar.
